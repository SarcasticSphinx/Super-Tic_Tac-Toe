How to Play Super Tic-Tac-Toe

Objective

The goal of Super Tic-Tac-Toe is to win by getting three of your marks in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, across the larger 3x3 grid of smaller 3x3 Tic-Tac-Toe boards.

Game Setup

The game is played on a larger 3x3 grid that consists of nine smaller 3x3 grids. Each player takes turns marking one of the inner boxes in an outer box.

Rules

  • Players take turns placing their mark (either '⭘' or '✘') in one of the inner boxes.
  • The outer box that corresponds to the inner box clicked becomes the selected box for the next turn.
  • If a player fills an outer box, they cannot play in that outer box again.
  • The game ends when one player has filled three outer boxes in a row, or when all boxes are filled without a winner (a draw).
  • Players can only play in the outer box indicated by the previous turn's inner box, unless that box is already filled.
