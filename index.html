Super Tic-Tac-Toe
How to Play?

Objective

Win by claiming three three consecutive outer boxes (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) on the large 3x3 grid. Each move inside an inner grid dictates the next grid for your opponent, adding a strategic twist to traditional Tic-Tac-Toe.

Rules

  • Players take turns placing their mark (⭘ or ✘) in one of the smaller boxes within the larger grid.
  • The position of your play in the small grid determines which large grid square your opponent must play in next.
  • Win a small grid by getting three in a row within it. This claims that square in the larger grid.
  • If your opponent sends you to a grid that's already won or full, you can play in any open grid.
  • Win the game by claiming three large grid squares in a row.
  • The game ends in a draw if all squares are filled without a winner.
